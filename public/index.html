🎯 Top BD Tricks
🔥 48-hour window
Contact within 48hrs of job post. Reply rate drops 70% after 1 week.
💰 Series A/B
Fresh budget + urgency. Best window: 0–90 days post-funding.
👥 Multi-thread
Email CTO + HR + VP Eng simultaneously. 3× reply rate.
